Data layers on the timeline


Rotation & orbits

IERS polar motion, length-of-day, and JPL DE441 ephemeris — measured, high precision.

Earthquakes & volcanoes

USGS M≥5 catalog and Smithsonian GVP eruption episodes, updated incrementally.

Space weather

Kp, Dst, solar wind, CMEs, and aurora from OMNI, NASA DONKI, and NOAA SWPC.

Plates & hotspots

PB2002 boundary kinematics and mantle hotspot positions, tectonic context for seismicity.
